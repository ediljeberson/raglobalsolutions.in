Innovative IT and ITES Solutions

RA Global Solutions, established in October 2022, is a leading provider of innovative IT and IT-enabled services (ITES). We are committed to helping businesses navigate the digital landscape with comprehensive solutions ranging from web and app development to medical billing, knowledge process outsourcing (KPO), and customer support.

Our internal projects include solutions like the Billing Management System, designed to streamline business operations and improve financial management processes.

We are headquartered at Nellore, where our team works on creating cutting-edge technologies and offering exceptional customer service.
